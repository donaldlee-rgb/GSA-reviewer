# GSA Services Agreement - Baseline Contract Template

**Date:** 23/2/26

## Overview
This is the standard services agreement template used as a baseline for contract review and redline analysis. All incoming contracts should be compared against this baseline to identify suggested changes.

## Key Contract Sections
1. Services
2. Payment and Taxes
3. Term
4. Rights to Deliverables
5. Confidentiality Obligations
6. Independent Contractor
7. Conflicting Services
8. Vendor Personnel
9. Vendor's Obligations
10. Indemnification
11. Limitation of Liability
12. Acceptance of Services
13. Vendor Cooperation
14. Insurance & Background Checks
15. Publicity
16. Extension of Agreement Terms to Customer's Affiliates
17. Audit
18. Miscellaneous

## Exhibits
- Background Check Policy
- Data Privacy Schedule Supplement
- Statement of Work Template
- Business Process Outsourcing Services

## Critical Clauses for Redline Review
- Section 4: Rights to Deliverables (IP ownership)
- Section 5: Confidentiality Obligations
- Section 10: Indemnification
- Section 14: Insurance Requirements
- Payment Terms (Section 2.a): 45 days net payment term
- Termination Rights (Section 3): At-will termination by Customer

## Purpose
Use this baseline template in contract_redline_analyzer.py to detect and categorize redlines in incoming vendor agreements.
